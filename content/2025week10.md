## News Summary

Week 10 delivered shocking upsets and thrilling finishes that dramatically reshaped the ACC landscape heading into the final stretch of the regular season.

These results significantly impact College Football Playoff positioning and conference championship implications.

**Ranked Resilience**: Virginia (No. 15) and Louisville (No. 16) navigated potential upset traps effectively. Virginia defeated California 31-21, while Louisville handled Virginia Tech 28-16, both teams maintaining their ranking momentum with convincing road victories.
