The #9 Fighting Irish delivered a statement performance with a commanding 37-15 victory over #22 Pittsburgh, showcasing their playoff credentials and reinforcing their status as a national title contender.

#19 Virginia continued their strong season with a convincing 34-17 win at Duke, demonstrating their offensive firepower and ability to win on the road during the critical stretch run.

**Miami Establishes Dominance**
#15 Miami delivered a dominant 41-7 performance against NC State, establishing themselves as a serious force in the ACC and sending a clear message to conference rivals.
